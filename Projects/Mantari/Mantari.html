MANTARI
Project Details
Project Type University/Team Project
Project Role(s) Lead Programer
Duriation 2 Weeks
Software(s) Unity | C#

Dive deep and explore the neon reef with the latest Mantari underwater ship model. Mantari is an on-rail shooter game as a part of the university's starfox inspired gamejam module that lasted 2 weeks. Mantari was Produced in a team of 6.

Mantari ranked 1st in best gameplay, most innovative and overall out of 13 entries within the university's module gamejam with team sizes varying for 5 to 10 members.
