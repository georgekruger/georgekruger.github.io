About Me

Hello, I’m George, an aspiring gameplay programmer and designer from the United Kingdom. I have been self-teaching coding since the age of 10, as well as software’s such as Blender, Photoshop, Vegas Pro and website coding, as well as crating this website. I’m a currently studying at the University of Portsmouth studying computer games technology. I have completed my first year of university and continuing on my second year

I am always working on gameplay concepts and projects to help broaden my knowledge and to continue to learn new things and overcoming any challenges, as I am fascinated in creating new and interesting gameplay concepts and mechanics.
